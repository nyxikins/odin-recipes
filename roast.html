Grammeowster's Roast

Are you hungry, dear?

Come sit by Grammeowster's hearth and have some of her hearty roast. It'll cure what ails you!

Ingredients

  • 10lb roast, best quality you can find
  • Salt
  • Blend of herbs and spices
  • Love

Steps

  1. Let the roast come to room temperature.
  2. Season generously with salt, herbs, and spices.
  3. Roast on spit over open fire until cooked through.
  4. Let rest. Carve. Serve with as many sides as you want, don't be shy!
  5. Best enjoyed warm and with friends.
