Fluffy Bunny Dango

Uu-ii-wa!

Pori nangu minga mitta niku de dano

Uzzute hinga de toete mune da~a~a

Domo iiru inwa de ta eta aki naaaa-(Hai!)

Sika dou da, nanao de ii

Ingredients

  • Rice flour mixed with uruchi rice flour and glutinous rice flour
  • Red bean paste
  • Sesame paste
  • Syrup made from shouyu (soy sauce), sugar, and starch

Steps

  1. Take the Monster Hunter's order - they always order the best food!
  2. Juggle the dango (this is essential)
  3. Skewer the dango, preferably while they are airborne.
  4. Catch the skewers on a serving platter.
  5. Garnish. Serve immediately with a steaming mug of tea!
